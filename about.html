About Dave

The Basics

I'm from a small town in Connecticut where I lived until I went to College in Illinois. Since then, I've lived in Montana, Florida, and just recently moved to the Detroit area. My wife and I live in Warren with our very cute one-year-old son and our very jealous dog.

My Interests

  • Coding

    • I've been teaching myself coding in my spare time over the last few months to get the sense of it and it's really fun! I really enjoy the challenge of running into new problems and figuring out how to fix them.

  • Writing

    • Before I started learning how to code, my go-to spare time activity was writing fiction. Storytelling is my passion. I'm excited for coding to be my day job so I can use my spare time for storytelling again!

  • Travel

    • I love to travel! Most recently, my wife and I travelled to Guam to visit her sister and brother-in-law and their new baby. My favorite place I've ever been is Israel.

  • Soccer

    • Speaking of interest in things overseas, I'm an avid soccer fan! My favorite team is Tottenham Hotspur who play in the English Premier League. They had their best season in a long time last year, but weren't able to win the league. I can't wait to catch my first Detroit City FC game!
